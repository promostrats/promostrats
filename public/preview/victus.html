377 Lordship Lane

This room has fed the Lane before.

Most restaurants have a story. Ours is that we've been in this building twice, fifty years apart.

1970s

Bursa Döner Kebab House

Lee's parents open one of the first kebab houses in South London. For a generation of East Dulwich, it's the first döner they ever eat.

2003

A stall in Greenwich

Victus & Bibo starts as a market stall — Latin for food and drink, and not much more than that to begin with.

2010s

Le Moulin, then nothing

An old French restaurant comes and goes. The building sits empty for years. At one point you could stand inside and see the sky.

2025

Lee comes home

Rebuilt from the basement up. Same address, same family — a Turkish kitchen and a low-lit bar, forty years on.

Still Turkish, still as good, and the welcome as warm. Tripadvisor · a customer of the original kebab house, 1970s
